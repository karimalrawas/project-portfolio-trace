About Parkour

Parkour is a physical discipline that involves overcoming obstacles and navigating through the environment in an efficient and creative way. It is a form of self-expression and challenges both the body and mind.

Parkour can be practiced anywhere, but it is particularly popular in urban environments where there are many obstacles to overcome.

Parkour Spots in Berlin

  • Tempelhof Park
  • Treptower Park
  • Mauerpark

Parkour Community in Berlin

The parkour community in Berlin is a group of dedicated athletes who come together to share their passion for parkour and push each other to new limits.

Join us at one of the spots listed above to see what parkour is all about and meet like-minded individuals.
